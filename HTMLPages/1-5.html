Almond Green Salad

Fresh and Crispy

Ingredients

1/2 pound bacon
2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
3 tablespoons honey
1/2 teaspoon dry hot mustard
1/2 teaspoon celery salt
1/2 teaspoon ground paprika
1/4 cup olive oil
1 head read leaf lettuce torn into bite-sized pices
1 (15 ounce) can of mandarin oranges, drained
1 bunch green onions, diced
3/4 cup slivered almonds

Directions

1. In medium skilley over medium-heat, cook bacon until evenly browned. Drain cool, and crumble.

2. To make the dressing, thourly blend the vinegar, honey, dry mustard, celery salt, paprika, and olive oil.

3. Place lettuce, oranges, green onions, bacon, and almonds in a serving bowl. Toss with dressing and serve.
